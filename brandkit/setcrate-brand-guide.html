02 — Clear Space
Minimum Clear Space
Maintain a clear zone around the logo equal to the height of the crate handle (the "H" unit). Never place text, graphics, or other logos within this boundary.

06 — Usage
Logo Dos & Don'ts
Protect the integrity of the SetCrate brand by following these guidelines when using the logo.
✓ Do
Use the logo on dark backgrounds with sufficient contrast
✗ Don't
Place the logo on busy or clashing color backgrounds
✓ Do
Use the light variant on white/light backgrounds
✗ Don't
Stretch, skew, rotate, or distort the logo in any way
✓ Do
Keep the icon and wordmark locked together at the correct proportion
✗ Don't
Change the brand colors — always use the purple gradient or monochrome
07 — Voice & Tone
Brand Voice
SetCrate speaks like a fellow producer — knowledgeable but never condescending, focused but never boring. The brand voice sits at the intersection of technical precision and creative energy.

Technical

We understand the tools. We speak the language of DAWs, sessions, and signal flow. No fluff, no marketing-speak. If it's a feature, we say what it does.

Direct

Short sentences. Active voice. We respect our users' time. If something takes three clicks, we say so. If it's free, we say that too.

Encouraging

We're here to help people make more music. The tone is always forward-looking — focused on what you can create, not what you're missing.
